The Story of Idris

The First Messenger of Allah

Everyone was living on Tawheed. Idris reminded the people to stay on Tawheed. Allah revelaed to Idris that he has the reward of everyone who does good. Idris saw one of the angels and asked him to tell the angel of death to increase his life. Jibril took Idris throughout the seven heavens. Idris and Jibril meet the angel of death in the fourth heaven. The angel of death was commanded by Allah to take the soul of Idris in the fourth heaven. The angel of death was not sure how that will happen since he was on the earth. Then Idris came to the fourth heaven and his soul was taken there. Allah mentions in the Quran that Idris is a truthful Messenger and he was raised on a high status.
